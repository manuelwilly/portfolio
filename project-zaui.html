Company Overview

Zaui Software is a leading reservation and management software company that provides cloud-based solutions for the tourism industry such as shuttle companies, tour operators, and activity suppliers to grow their online bookings and streamline their operations.

Work Experience

I started working full-time at Zaui on December 2019 as a sole UX/UI designer. At the company, my role was split between WBE (web booking engine) and the back-office integrations team. For most of the time, I worked on the WBE team and focused on building and improving features for our new online booking management platform to help customers in simplifying their booking process and checkout flow.

My day-to-day duties included collaborating with project managers and developers to help guide and bring designs to our customers. I was also responsible for guiding features from research, interface design, protoyping, presenting designs to our team, usability testing, and design-to-development handoff.

The rest of my work is still under an NDA, but I would be willing to discuss my experience and takeaways in-person or at an interview.
